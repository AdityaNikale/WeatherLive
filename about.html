👋 About WeatherLive

WeatherLive is a modern, responsive, and lightweight weather forecast application built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap. It is designed to provide real-time weather information with an engaging and beautiful interface.

This app uses the OpenWeatherMap API to fetch accurate weather data and includes dynamic features such as dark/light mode, motivational quotes, animated weather icons, and smooth transitions.

Whether you’re planning your next trip or just curious about the climate, WeatherLive is your perfect companion — simple, elegant, and functional.
